Project management

I was part of project team which was responsible for creating new product in Factoring department portfolio.
My role was to create and update excel sheet to calculate rating for all levels of local governments in Poland. Data was imported from Ministry of Finance which provided public files in csv format quarterly with financial data. Rating was calculated based on financial indicators and presented for selected time period. This calculation allowed to make decision about level of financing for selected municipal. I was also preparing documentation for this product.
